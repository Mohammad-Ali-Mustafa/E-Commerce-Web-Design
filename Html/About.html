What We Do


Rheem Cooling is a specialist Air Conditioner retail store. Founded in 1998,
Rheem Cooling brings under one roof, multi-brand air conditioning products.



We have over 20 brands in more than 40 stores across.
Rheem Cooling brings alive the promise of a Brighter Every Day with a world-class ambience to shop both in-store and online,
while also creating a seamless omnichannel shopping experience that lets you enjoy the best of both, online and offline worlds.
You will always be spoilt for choice at Rheem Cooling but will never be left confused.
Experts at our stores will help you find the right Air Conditioner for your needs.
